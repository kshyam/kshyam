### 🚀 About Me

I started my tech journey as a **Ruby on Rails Developer**, but my deep passion for containerization, orchestration, and infrastructure scalability naturally guided me into the cloud ecosystem. Today, I bridge the gap between development and operations as a **Cloud, SRE, and DevOps Engineer**. 

- 🐳 **Containerization Architect**: Love dismantling monoliths and packaging them cleanly into Docker & Kubernetes.
- 🛠️ **Automation Evangelist**: Firm believer that if you have to do it twice, it should be automated.
- 💡 **Backend Savvy**: Highly experienced with Python, Flask APIs, relational databases, and Cloud Storage integration.
- 🤝 **Open Source**: Active member & contributor across organizations like `@CodeCrux` and `@razorops-public`.
